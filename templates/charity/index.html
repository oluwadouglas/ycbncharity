About YCBN

Empowering Youth, Strengthening Communities, Driving Change

The Youth Capacity Building Network (YCBN) equips young Ugandans with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities to lead in their communities. Through innovative programs and strategic partnerships, we create clear pathways to education, employment, and civic leadership.

Program Areas

Our Three Core Areas of Impact

Skills Development

Comprehensive training in digital literacy, entrepreneurship, leadership, and technical skills that prepare youth for today‚Äôs workforce.

Community Projects

Supporting youth-led initiatives that address local challenges through funding, mentorship, and resource mobilization.

Mentorship and Networking

Connecting young leaders with experienced mentors and building networks for collaboration and career advancement.

  • Delivering practical training in leadership and entrepreneurship
  • Providing mentorship and meaningful networking opportunities
  • Facilitating access to funding and resources for youth-led initiatives
