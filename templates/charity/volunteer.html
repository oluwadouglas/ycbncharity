Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

To become a volunteer, simply fill out our volunteer application form on the contact page. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you about available opportunities that match your skills and interests.

We welcome volunteers with all kinds of skills and backgrounds. Whether you're good with people, have technical skills, or just want to help, there's a place for you. Specific roles may require certain skills, but many of our opportunities are open to anyone with a willingness to learn and help.

We have opportunities that can fit any schedule. Some volunteers contribute a few hours a week, while others help with one-time events. During the application process, you can discuss your availability with our volunteer coordinator to find the right fit for you.
